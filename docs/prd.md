# Amman Smart Platform Requirements Document

## 1. Application Overview

### 1.1 Application Name
Amman Smart Platform

### 1.2 Application Description
A comprehensive digital platform dedicated to Amman Smart City initiatives, providing citizens and visitors with access to smart city services, information, and resources to enhance urban living experience in Amman.

## 2. Core Features

### 2.1 Smart City Information Hub
- Display comprehensive information about Amman's smart city projects and initiatives
- Showcase smart infrastructure developments and technological advancements
- Provide updates on ongoing and planned smart city programs

### 2.2 Service Directory
- List available smart city services for residents and visitors
- Categorize services by type (transportation, utilities, public services, etc.)
- Provide access points and usage instructions for each service

### 2.3 City Data Dashboard
- Present real-time city data and statistics
- Display key performance indicators for smart city initiatives
- Visualize urban metrics and trends

### 2.4 News and Updates
- Publish latest news about Amman Smart City developments
- Share announcements and important notifications
- Highlight success stories and achievements

### 2.5 Contact and Support
- Provide contact information for inquiries
- Offer support channels for platform users
- Include feedback mechanism for continuous improvement
